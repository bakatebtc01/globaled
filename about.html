About Global-Ed

Global-Ed is an international education facilitation agency supporting PNG students to access global STEM opportunities.

Founder & Director: Jayson Kopeap.

Government role: Key consultant/advisor to PNG National Department of Education STEM scholarship initiatives.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Connect Papua New Guinea’s brightest students with global STEM education.

Vision: Become PNG’s leading international education gateway and scale across the Pacific.

Built for mobile access first, with resilient user flows for intermittent connectivity.
